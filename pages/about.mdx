# About

LA Makers United Against COVID-19 is spearheaded by the Collaborative Research Association of Social Hacktivity (CRASH). CRASH is a 501(c)3 nonprofit formed by a group of volunteer engineers, programmers, builders, scientists, teachers, and artists who like to solve problems and explore new ideas.  Since our founding in 2009, CRASH has promoted accessibility in science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics (STEAM) by teaching classes, doing outreach at public events, providing online resources, and running a shared workshop.
